## STARTING KNOWLEDGE (Pre-Round 1)

### Who Elspeth Is
- Name: Elspeth Crane
- Cover: Features writer, New York Tribune — genuine cover, she is actually a journalist
- Real role: British Naval Intelligence operative — tasked to confirm the Ledger exists and photograph at least one part of it
- Abilities: Press Access (once per game), Profile Read (after one round of observation), Darkroom (develop film in the ship's photographic studio — she has the key)
- Items: Miniature camera concealed in cigarette case; photographic studio key; press credentials; shorthand notebook
- Goal: Confirm the Ledger exists AND photograph Part One or Part Two without being caught or exposed

### What Elspeth Knows Going In
- She has been told by her handler in London that a man named CORNELIUS VANTRANI is believed to possess a document called "the Ledger" — a comprehensive record of Western intelligence operatives.
- She does not know if the Ledger is real. Her first job is to confirm it exists.
- She has been told she has one ally aboard — but not who. (She does not know it is Henry Ashby.)
- She knows the ship docks Saturday morning. She has four nights.
- She is aware that other parties may be interested in whatever Vantrani is holding.
- She does NOT know: the Ledger's structure (two parts, two locations), who has been offered it, the identities of any other operatives aboard.

### Characters Known (Starting)
- **Cornelius Vantrani** — The host. 70, shipping magnate, Austro-Hungarian émigré. She has a professional relationship with him — he agreed to the Tribune profile piece. She knows he is charming, powerful, and careful with information.
- All other guests: seen briefly at arrival. No detailed knowledge yet.

### Locations Known (Starting)
- **Grand Salon** — Where she is. Ballroom, bar, orchestra, social center of first class.
- **Dining Room** — She knows it exists. Has not been there yet this evening.
- **Stateroom Corridor** — She knows where her room is (Stateroom 4).

### Plot Knowledge (Starting)
- Vantrani may possess a document of significant intelligence value called the Ledger.
- She is here to confirm this and, if possible, photograph it.
- She has an unknown ally aboard.

### Items Held
