## SCENE STRUCTURE
The climax should unfold as a cascade of simultaneous events:
1. Felicity makes her move — public challenge
2. This creates noise and distraction
3. Kwame slips away
4. Cassius begins to descend the stage
5. August sees a gap (or doesn't, depending on Harlow)
6. Soren moves toward the corridor where Cassius might go
7. Eris reaches Yuki demanding answers
8. The player must choose what to deal with

The scene should feel like the world spinning out of control — too many things happening at once, the player forced to choose what matters most. This is the heart of the game.
